About Us

IT ALL STARTED NOT IN A DAY, BUT OVER YEARS OF FACING...

Frustrating hours spent on cooking, irregularity by maids, expensive food from online delivery services and lack of homely and hygienic food. So we asked ourselves how to get over these issues so that we can focus on our hobbies and family time without having to think a lot about our daily meals.

So we started GerryEats, a startup striving to autopilot your lunch and dinner, so that it hardly takes a few minutes of your day to have healthy nutritious food at the same price point. With the facility of a subscription service and the option of choosing among various food items, we will try to make your meals as diverse and homely as possible.

Menu

We bring to you homely Indian dishes which feels exactly like your home cooked food. We keep rotating the menu everyday so that you don't get bored of eating it. You curate your own meal under the same price roof.
